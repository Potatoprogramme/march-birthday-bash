Summer Games
1
🖼️ "4 Pics, 1 Word — Summer Edition"
Four summer-themed pictures are shown on screen (or printed on paper) and players guess the one word that connects them all — done entirely from their seats! Examples: 🌊🏄🐚👙 = BEACH · 🌞😎🧴🕶️ = SUMMER · 🍦🥭🥤🍉 = COLD. First team to shout the correct answer scores a point. Prepare 5–7 rounds for non-stop fun! 🏆
Game Master: MARK
2
🎬 "Summer Charades — Office Edition"
One player stands at their spot and acts out a summer-themed word — no talking, no sounds, just body language! Everyone else guesses from their seats. Words include: sunburn, eating halo-halo, building sandcastles, getting stung by a jellyfish, putting on sunscreen, riding a bangka. The team with the most correct guesses wins! 🌴
