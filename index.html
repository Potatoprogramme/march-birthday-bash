MARCHBASH!

Birthday Celebration Program

⏱ Approximately 15–30 minutes
🙏
Opening Prayer
We begin with a moment of gratitude and blessings for our March celebrants.
Led by: JAMES
🎭
Recognition of March Birthday Celebrants
Each celebrant is called one by one — but here's the twist: instead of a normal introduction, the emcee reads a "dramatic telenovela narration" of their life story (exaggerated and made up on the spot). The celebrant must react dramatically to every line! 🎬
🎁 Token Suggestion
A personalized "Birthday Survival Kit" — a small resealable bag containing: a candy (for sweetness), a coin (for prosperity), a small note with a handwritten wish from the team, and a rubber band (to keep it all together). Cheap to make but deeply personal. 💛
🎮
Games
→ "Birthday Lie Detector" — Each celebrant shares 2 truths and 1 lie about themselves. The crowd votes on which one is the lie!
Game Master: MARK
→ "Hula Hula Birthday Edition" — Pass a hula hoop through a line of people without letting go of hands. The team that finishes fastest wins a treat!
Game Master: TRISHIA
· · ·
🕯️
Blowing of Candles
All March celebrants gather together for a group candle blowing. One big wish for all! 🎂
✨
Blessing of the Food
Led by: SIR JR
🍽️
Eating Time
Dig in and enjoy! Celebrants eat first. 🥳
📸
March Memories Photo Presentation
A short slideshow of photos from team highlights and moments this March.
🎤
Intermission Number & Sing Along
End the bash on a high note! Everyone joins in for a sing-along birthday surprise. 🎶
🎉 Happy Birthday, March Celebrants!
