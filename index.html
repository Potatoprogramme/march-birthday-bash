MARCHBEACH BASH!

🎂 Monthly Birthday Celebration Program 🎂

⏱ 15–30 minutes  ·  Tara na sa celebration!
🙏
Opening Prayer
We kick off with a prayer of gratitude and blessings for all our March beachcombers — err, celebrants!
Led by: JAMES
🎭
Recognition of March Birthday Celebrants
Each celebrant walks up to receive their recognition — but the emcee introduces them as if they're a famous summer tourist spot.

e.g., "Ladies and gentlemen, welcome to [Name] — known for its warm personality, unpredictable weather, and zero entrance fee!" 🏝️

The crowd rates each "tourist spot" by waving their hands like a wave! 🌊
🎁 Summer Token — "Beach Survival Kit"
A small resealable bag tied with a raffia ribbon containing:
🌞 Sunscreen sachet — "for a bright year ahead"
🍬 Candy — "because life should be sweet"
🪙 Coin — "for good fortune"
📝 Handwritten note from the team — the real treasure

Cheap to make (~₱25–35) but full of summer heart! 💛
🎮
Summer Games
1
🕵️ "Hot Sand, Don't Stop!" (Musical Chairs — Summer Remix)
Play a fun OPM summer song. Whoever is left standing when it stops has to answer a fun summer question — like "Describe your dream beach in 5 words!" Last person seated wins a treat. ☀️
Game Master: MARK
2
🌊 "Summer Charades Beach Edition"
Teams act out summer-related words and phrases — sunburn, making sandcastles, getting stung by a jellyfish, eating halo-halo in the heat — no words, only actions! Most correct guesses wins! 🦀
Game Master: TRISHIA
